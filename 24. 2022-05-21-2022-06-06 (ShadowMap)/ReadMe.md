### Camera Movement  
**[WASD]**: Moving the camera.            
**[LeftMouseDown]**: Camera Rotation.    
### ShadowMap  
**[RightArrow, LeftArrow, UpArrow, DownArrow]**: ShadowMap X/Z movement.

**[X]**: Changes to **SOLID MODE**
